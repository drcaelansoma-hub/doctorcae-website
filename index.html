About Me

Supporting children, teens, and the adults who love them.

I am a clinical psychologist with over 25 years of experience helping children and the adults who love them. I strive to make complex topics such as brain science, child development, emotions, and behavior easy to understand. I help families balance warmth with structure by using simple analogies, visuals and practical strategies.

I have written books, created curricula and taught thousands of professionals about stress, trauma, resilience and social-emotional development in children. I am currently in private practice in Michigan, where I continue the work I love. I work with children and families feel calmer, more confident, and connected. I am also a mom of two teenage daughters.
