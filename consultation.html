Parent Consultation

A calm, focused starting point when things feel overwhelming

If your child is struggling with big emotions, frequent meltdowns, shutdown, anxiety, or behavior that feels out of proportion, this consultation gives us a place to slow down and make sense of what's happening — together.

This is a parent-only session designed to help you understand your child's nervous system, clarify next steps, and leave with practical guidance you can use right away.

Who This Consultation Is For

This session is a good fit if you are:

  • Feeling stuck or unsure how to respond to your child's behavior
  • Noticing frequent emotional dysregulation at home or school
  • Wondering whether therapy is the right next step
  • Wanting professional guidance without committing to ongoing sessions yet

What We'll Cover During Our Time Together

During this one-hour consultation, we will:

  • Discuss your child's history, current concerns, and stressors
  • Identify patterns in behavior through a regulation and nervous-system lens
  • Explore what may be driving your child's reactions beneath the behavior
  • Talk through practical, developmentally appropriate strategies for support

What You'll Receive After the Session

Following our consultation, you will receive:

  • A written summary of key themes and observations
  • Clear, individualized recommendations and next steps
  • Guidance on whether ongoing therapy, parent support, or other resources may be helpful
